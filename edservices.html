Educational Services

Academic Tutoring

The Seiji’s Bridge teaching method is based on a child centered differentiated learning theory, specific to a student’s needs. We take into consideration the following attributes of an individual’s academic needs: active/reflective, sensing/intuitive, verbal/visual, sequential/global learning with emphasis placed on auditory, visual, and kinesthetic-motor processing.

Social-Emotional Learning Skills

Stressful school environments can lead to anxiety and depression, there can be multiple factors to these conditions. At Seiji’s Bridge, we help individuals learn coping skills to improve interpersonal relationships. We provide guidance to help understand how individuals can be apart of a group and feel emotionally safe in social situations.

General and Special Education Teacher's Workshops

We hold workshops to provide continuing education on current methods when working with students on the autism spectrum, with learning differences, dyslexia, and Attention Deficit-Hyperactivity Disorder. Workshops can be scheduled annually or by the interest of a school.

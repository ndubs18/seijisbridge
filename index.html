Educational Services

Provide Academic Tutoring and Social-Emotional Learning Skills for Students and Adults with Autism Spectrum Disorders, Depression, Anxiety, Attention Deficit-Hyperactivity Disorder, and other developmental differences. Conduct Workshops for General & Special Education Teachers.

Counseling

Provide counseling for Individuals and families of children with Autism Spectrum Disorders, Depression, Anxiety, Attention Deficit-Hyperactivity Disorder, and other developmental differences. Specialized in Non-Verbal counseling therapies: Play, sand tray, art, and mindfulness therapy techniques.

Learn More
Societal Education

Help individuals with one on one skills to be apart of group settings and become more confident interacting in societal social situations. Creating social groups for individuals with Autism Spectrum Disorders, intellectual, and developmental differences.

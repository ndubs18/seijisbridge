Counseling

Non-Verbal Counseling Therapies

Non-verbal counseling services includes play, art, and sand tray therapy techniques. Play therapy techniques leads to expressive feelings and emotions with puppets, books, and sensory toys.

Individuals and Families

Solution Focused, Narrative and Mindfulness therapies for individuals and families. Solution Focused therapy is goal oriented in finding solutions in the present and future.

Specific Counseling

Understand marked impairments of eye-to-eye gaze, facial expression and body postures. We have awareness of psychomotor agitation and tearfulness.
